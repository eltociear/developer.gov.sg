Overview

The DSA recognises government agencies, organisations, and citizens who have upheld and contributed to exceptional digital service standards. Held annually since 2021, it is co-organised by GovTech and the Smart Nation Digital Government Office (SNDGO) and serves as a platform for digital services to be publicly recognised for their excellence.

For the first time this year, new categories have been introduced to the DSA to recognise digital service excellence in the private sector as well as citizen contributions.

Who should attend: All professionals involved in cybersecurity projects; Levelling: Open to all
