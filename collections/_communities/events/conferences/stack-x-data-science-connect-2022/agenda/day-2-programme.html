Virtual Conference: Zoom Webinar

[Data Leader Dialogue] Beyond Dashboards: Evolve Your Experiences with Data and Analytics

While it gets easier to build a dashboard, this can result in multiple challenges, such as fragmented governance and implementation, inconsistent reports, and higher maintenance needs. How can data leaders look beyond charts and dashboards, support your teams to shift from traditional approaches, and embed data in every decision? Data Leaders Dialogue is a forum for data and analytics leaders to hear from industry players and discuss common challenges in the public sector. This is an invite-only event.
