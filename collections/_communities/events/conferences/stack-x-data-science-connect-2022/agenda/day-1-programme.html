Afternoon Tracks (With Live Q&A)

In the afternoon, we have an exciting line-up of speakers across 3 concurrent tracks. These tracks will be led by technical experts on topics such as Data Transformation, Data Products, Data Engineering, AI Applications and Video Analytics. Each track will offer attendees the opportunity to interact with speakers through live Q&A sessions. Find out more about each track below.

Track 1: Data Science & Data Transformation

Join this track to learn how Government agencies are tackling challenges when it comes to Data Science and Data Transformation at the organisational level. Speakers will be sharing on some of the best practices they have adopted as transformation enablers.
