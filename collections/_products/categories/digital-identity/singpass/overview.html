Singpass, Singapore’s National Digital Identity (NDI), is a strategic national project implemented and managed by the Government Technology Agency (GovTech) under the Smart Nation Digital Government Group (SNDGG). SNDGG was formed in 2017 and is housed under the Prime Minister’s Office (PMO).

As a foundational digital infrastructure that is critical to achieving Singapore’s vision of improving the lives of citizens, Singpass creates opportunities for businesses, and transforms the capabilities of government agencies. It provides government agencies and private sector organisations with a convenient and secure platform for users to conduct transactions.

As of February 2022, over 700 organisations offering more than 2,000 services have adopted Singpass. This means, Singpass boosts over 4.5 million users, which consists of 97% of Singapore Citizens and Permanent Residents aged 15 and above. To add to this, more than 350 million personal and corporate transactions are facilitated by Singpass every year.
