Factsheets

  • Singapore Digital Government Journey: Learn how GovTech has led digital transformation within the Singapore government to help materialise our Smart Nation vision.
  • API Exchange (APEX): Learn more about APEX, a centralised and secure government API gateway, that facilitates data driven decision making and cross-agency collaboration through APIs.
  • GoBusiness: Learn about our GoBusiness platform, otherwise known as the go-to platform for businesses in Singapore to access over 300 Government e-services and resources (grants, regulatory information, business recommendations etc).
  • Government on Commercial Cloud (GCC): Learn more about GCC, a “wrapper” platform that provides government agencies with a consistent means of adopting commercial cloud solutions (e.g., Amazon Web Services (AWS), Azure and Google Cloud).
  • LifeSG: Learn more about LifeSG, our national platform for accessing over 100 government services for citizens. This factsheet covers the LifeSG mobile app, and how personalised recommendations are provided to users based on their profiles and preferences.
  • Singapore Government Tech Stack (SGTS): Learn more about SGTS, a set of platform tools that enables code reuse and streamlines and simplifies the development process across the government. This allows government agencies to build secure, high-quality applications.
  • Singpass: This factsheet provides key details about Singpass, Singapore’s National Digital Identity.

Case Studies

  • National Digital Identity and Government Data Sharing in Singapore - Full case study and Abridged case study: This is a case study of Singapore’s National Digital Identity, Singpass, and APEX. This is a collaboration between GovTech and the World Bank.
